About The Fest

Welcome to NEXUS TECH 2K26 — the Technical Symposium hosted by Asian College of Engineering and Technology, where the brightest innovators from across the nation converge to battle for glory on 17 March 2026.

Like the Avengers assembling against a universe-scale threat, NEXUS TECH 2K26 brings together the sharpest engineers, coders, designers, and creative minds. This is not just an event — it is your Endgame.

"Whatever it takes." — NEXUS TECH 2K26 is your arena to prove you have what it takes to shape tomorrow's technology.

Organized by the AI&DS Department, NEXUS TECH 2K26 features Technical challenges, Non-Technical competitions, and Cultural events across a single power-packed day of innovation, disruption, and glory.
